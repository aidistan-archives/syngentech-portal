Synthetic Biology

Synthetic biology, as one of the most popular frontiers, is an interdisciplinary branch of biology and engineering aiming to design and construct new biological parts, devices, and systems, or redesign existing natural biological systems for useful purposes. Nowadays, synthetic biology has been applied to a number of areas such as health, energy, the environment, agriculture, etc.

SyngenTech

SyngenTech is a biotechnology company focused on applying synthetic biology to scientific research and public healthcare. Since its establishment, SyngenTech has been committed to providing researchers around the world with high-quality, innovative technical services and products. Its services and products，as created by scientists for scientists, are designed to cover the whole industrial chain for synthetic biology, including various bioreagents, a sharing repository for genetic elements, high-quality technical services and clinical healthcare.
